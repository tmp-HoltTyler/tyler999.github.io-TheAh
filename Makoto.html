Makoto

Makoto is the sixth member of the Phantom Thieves. She has brown hair in a bob cut style with a headband in a matching color and abnormally red eyes.
For one of her school uniforms she wears black and red pleated skirt, black pantyhose and blackish brown ankle boots.
Her phantom theif outfit consists of a dark blue and black skintight suit with a long black scarf,
a dark gray leather breastplate, shoulder/knee pads with spikes and boots that metal
on the heels and toes, white gloves, and an iron mask.
At her school, she was a student
council president.

    Code name: Queen


    Persona: Johanna
