Futaba

Futaba is the seventh member of the Phantom Thieves. She is a young girl with hip length dyed light orange hair with bangs, and brown eyes with a faint purple tone.
She also wear black glasses. One of her outfits consists of an off-shoulder white top with a red splatter design on her chest with five white asterisks,
a dark green jacket with a faux fur hood, black short shorts, black thigh highs socks and black knee-high boots with buckles,
etc. Her phantom thief outfits consists of a skintight black bodysuit that has neon green glowing strips,
black boots with neon green soles, and a pair of big black goggles with orange lenses as a mask.
She is a very skilled hacker that greatly improved
the power of the Phantom Thieves.
