Ann

Ann is the forth member of the Phantom Thieves. She has light blue eyes, blonde, wavy hair with bangs.
She wears a modified verison of the winter uniform.
Her Phantom theif outfit consists of a red latex catsuit that is skin tight, pink gloves, red thigh-high boots, and a red panther mask.
She can be a very selfless girl with compassion.
