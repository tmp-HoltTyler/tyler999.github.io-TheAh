Yusuke

The fifth member of the Phantom Thieves. He has dark blue hair with some of it covering his left eye with grey eyes.
He does not go to the schoo that the other phantom thieves attend. One of his uniforms consists of a white gakuran-style shirt, black pants and black loafers.
His phantom thief attire consists of kitsune-style(half)mask, a black high-collared jumpsuit with a striped sash
worn across his hips with a white fox tail attached, white boots,
and light blue gloves.
He has an extremely strong passion with art.

    Code name: Fox


    Persona: Goemon


    Ultimate persona: Kamu Susano-o


    Arcana: The Emperor
