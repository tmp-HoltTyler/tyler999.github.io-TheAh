Morgana

Morgana is a major charater you will meet in Persona 5.
He is a black cat with blue eyes and wears a yellow scarf.
Him and the protagonist made a deal with
each other to help each other out. He is the one who keeps the Phantom Thieves moving.
Morgana suffered from amnesia and wishes to regain
all of his past memories which he think the
depths of Mementos has.

    Code name: Mona


    Persona: Zorro


    Ultimate persona: Mercurius


    Arcana: The Magician


    Goal: Uncover the mystery of mementos, regain memories
