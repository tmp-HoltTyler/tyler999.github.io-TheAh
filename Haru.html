Haru

Haru is the eighth to be a part of the Phantom thieves. She has short but chin-length curly hair and has around the same eye color as Futaba.
One of her outfits is a white dress shirt with one dark blue ribbon, pale green coat with a fluffy texture,
a pink skirt, and white tights with brown shoes. Her phantom thief outfit is a dark purple cavalier
hat with a feather tucked into a purple hatband, a long-sleeved pink blouse
with a white neck jabot, a black vest with gold buttons, black pantyhose,
a holster belt, and black and violet gloves and mask.
She has a strong passion for gardening.

    Code name: Noir


    Persona: Milady


    Ultimate persona: Astarte


    Arcana: The Empress
